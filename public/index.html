Questions

Anything else? Let's clear it up.

Will my posts end up sounding like AI?+

Never — because Sona doesn't write for you. You write. Sona surrounds you with the right raw material: proven outliers to remix, hook templates to drop in, and your own style notes pinned beside the editor. The output stays unmistakably you. (Optional AI-assist arrives later as a separate paid add-on — not part of the founding offer.)

Which platforms does it support?+

X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Threads, Instagram, Substack, and YouTube descriptions. We auto-reformat per platform so you don't have to.

Where do the "outlier" posts come from?+

We track over a million high-performing posts across the supported platforms, refreshed daily. Outliers are detected by comparing performance to a creator's baseline — not just raw view counts.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes. Every paid plan includes 7 days free with no card required. The Starter plan is free forever.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Anytime, one click, no questions. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

Are my drafts and style notes private?+

Yes — completely. Your drafts, saved ideas, and playbook live in your workspace, encrypted at rest, never sent anywhere or shared with other users. We don't sell data, we don't train anything on your writing.
